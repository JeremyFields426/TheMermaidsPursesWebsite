Mermaid's Purses Info

Reproduction is not only a fundamental component of many basic biological processes, it also has important management and conservation implications.

Oviparous (egg laying) elasmobranch species (sharks and skates) are particularly well suited for examining reproductive cycles because laid eggs are easily identifiable. This application will give husbandry professionals a platform to store egg laying habits of their females, as well as generate analytic reports on the reproduction tendincies for elasmobranchs in their care. Thank you for contributing your data to our site!
